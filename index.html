About Me

My name is Nittaya and I'm a University of Connecticut class of 2003 alumni with a background in Genetic Sciences. I spent many years working in labs including at Bayer Pharmaceutical and Yale University School of Medicine DNA Lab as an intern, Dianon Systems as a molecular technologist and at Lonza Biologics as a QC analyst. I lived overseas in Taiwan for many years and while there I took courses to learn Mandarin Chinese and immersed myself in the culture to become fully fluent in the language. I've recently relocated back to the US and am currently enrolled in the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp through the University of Connecticul School of Engineering in partnership with edX. I hope to find a new career as a coder that can combine with my work experience in molecular science. In my free time I enjoy running, hike and cycling with my family.
